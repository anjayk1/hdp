What's your chest pain type?

Type 1: Typical Angina (Chest, arm, or jaw pain that feels dull, heavy, tight, or crushing)
Type 2: Atypical Angina (Epigastric or back pain, burning/stabbing feeling, or indigestion)
Type 3: Non-Anginal Pain (Heartburn, regurgitation, pressure or squeezing pain behind the breast bone, neck, back, or left arm )
Type 4: Asymptomatic (No Pain or None of the ones described above)
